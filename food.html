Breakfast

Fluffy Pancakes

Start your day with these light and fluffy pancakes.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
Instructions:
  1. Mix dry ingredients. In another bowl, whisk wet ingredients.
  2. Combine and mix until just blended.
  3. Cook on a greased skillet until golden brown.

Avocado Toast

Healthy and delicious, ready in minutes.

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash avocado and spread on toast.
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.

Lunch

Grilled Chicken Salad

Fresh greens with juicy grilled chicken and a tangy vinaigrette.

Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • Vinaigrette dressing
Instructions:
  1. Grill chicken and slice.
  2. Toss greens, tomatoes, and cheese in a bowl.
  3. Top with chicken and drizzle with vinaigrette.

Veggie Wrap

A quick, nutritious lunch packed with fresh veggies.

Ingredients:
  • 1 large tortilla
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
Instructions:
  1. Spread hummus on tortilla.
  2. Layer veggies and roll tightly.
  3. Slice and serve.

Dinner

Spaghetti Bolognese

Classic Italian pasta with rich meat sauce.

Ingredients:
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp Italian herbs
Instructions:
  1. Cook spaghetti as per package.
  2. Brown beef and onion in a pan.
  3. Add sauce and herbs, simmer 10 min.
  4. Serve sauce over pasta.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Colorful veggies sautéed in a savory sauce.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a wok, add garlic.
  2. Add veggies and stir fry 5 min.
  3. Add soy sauce, cook 2 min more.

Desserts

Chocolate Brownies

Rich, fudgy brownies for chocolate lovers.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
Instructions:
  1. Melt butter, mix with sugar and eggs.
  2. Add cocoa and flour, mix well.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 20-25 min.

Fruit Parfait

Light and refreshing layers of fruit and yogurt.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Honey to taste
Instructions:
  1. Layer yogurt, berries, and granola in a glass.
  2. Drizzle with honey and serve chilled.
